## What Is This?

Unstable Rifts is a Hytale server mod that transforms the game into a replayable top-down dungeon shooter.

Instead of a simple one-off dungeon, each run throws your party into a generated combat route filled with enemies, branching rooms, loot crates, shops, and a boss at the end. You fight with a full set of ranged weapons, melee options, elemental effects, and armor builds while trying to survive long enough to push deeper.

It is built to be easy to understand for new players, but strong on replayability once you start chasing better drops, stronger builds, and cleaner runs with friends.

## Story

Orbis is under attack.

The **Industrial faction** has invaded the world to strip it of its resources. Their first major target is the **Kweebecs**. Villages are being burned, homes are being destroyed, and captured Kweebecs are being enslaved or infected by an unknown plague.

That plague twists former allies into **DeadWood**.

Your job is to enter the unstable rifts left by the invaders, clear their strongholds, and stop the Kweebecs from being fully overrun. Every dungeon you finish is part of that fight.

## Why playing

1. **Top-down shooter combat** with guns, melee weapons, elemental effects, and fast room-to-room fights.
2. **19 weapons with very different feel**, from pistols and rifles to muskets, blunderbusses, tasers, and summoning weapons.
3. **Generated dungeon routes** with main paths, side branches, challenge rooms, shops, treasure rooms, and boss encounters.
4. **Co-op replayability** built around party play, shared coins, revives, reconnect support, and loot progression.
5. **Armor build variety** through multiple sets, set bonuses, and different stat profiles.
6. **Roguelike pacing** where each run gives you better loot, tougher enemies, and a strong reason to go again.

## How Do I Start Playing?

You can begin a run in three main ways:

1. **Find an Ancient Party Portal** in a Kweebec village.
2. **Craft your own portal** and use it wherever you want.
3. **Open the party menu with `/ur party ui`** to create or manage a party directly.

Once your party is ready, invite friends, open the party interface, and start the run. If you prefer commands, you can also use `/ur party invite <player>` and `/ur party start`.

## What Can You Do In A Run?

- Explore a generated dungeon with branching paths.
- Fight Industrial enemies, infected DeadWood, wildlife, and bosses.
- Break crates for guns, armor, ammo, healing, and coins.
- Buy upgrades in safe shop rooms.
- Revive teammates when things go wrong.
- Beat the boss and push back the invasion one rift at a time.

## Main Commands

- `/ur party ui` opens the party interface and is the easiest way to start.
- `/ur party invite <player>` invites a friend.
- `/ur party start` launches the dungeon run.
- `/ur topcamera` rotates the top-down camera.

For the full command list, see the [wiki command page](https://wiki.hytalemodding.dev/mod/unstable-rifts/commands-1).

## Installation

1. Download the latest release jar.
2. Put it in your server `mods/` folder.
3. Restart the server.

The mod ships with its own assets, so players can jump in and start using the new content right away.
