# Lore

Unstable Rifts takes place during a violent invasion of Orbis. A new enemy faction, known as the **Industrial faction**, has entered the world to seize land, power, and natural resources.

They are not here to live beside the people of Orbis. They are here to take what they want and leave ruin behind.

---

## The Invasion of Orbis

The Industrial faction arrived through unstable rifts and began spreading across Orbis. Their machines, soldiers, and Hazmat units pushed into peaceful lands and turned them into war zones.

Their goal is simple: take the world's resources, crush resistance, and keep expanding.

---

## The Kweebecs Under Attack

The first friendly faction to suffer the full force of this invasion is the **Kweebecs**.

Their villages are being attacked, burned, and torn apart. Homes are destroyed, sacred places are ruined, and many Kweebecs are captured and forced into slavery.

What was once a lively and peaceful place is now full of smoke, broken buildings, and fear.

---

## The Plague

As the invasion spreads, something even worse begins to happen.

An unknown plague starts infecting Kweebecs and changing them into **DeadWood**. These creatures were once allies, but the plague twists them into hostile enemies.

Right now, there is no cure.

That is why players are forced to fight DeadWood inside the dungeon. They are victims of the invasion, but they can no longer be saved in their current state.

---

## Why the Rifts Matter

The Industrial faction leaves rifts behind as it pushes deeper into Orbis. These rifts become dangerous battle zones filled with enemy forces, infected creatures, traps, and stolen resources.

Each dungeon run is an attack on one of those footholds.

When you enter a rift, you are stepping into enemy territory.

---

## Your Goal

Your job is to stop the invasion before the Kweebecs are completely wiped out.

To do that, you must:

1. Enter the rifts left by the Industrial faction.
2. Fight through their forces and the infected DeadWood.
3. Defeat the boss at the end of the dungeon.
4. Break the enemy hold over that area of Orbis.

Every completed dungeon is a step toward saving the Kweebecs from total invasion.

---

## Why You Fight Former Allies

One of the sad parts of Unstable Rifts is that not every enemy was always an enemy.

Some of the creatures you fight were once Kweebecs or part of the life around them. The plague and the invasion changed them.

That gives the dungeon its tone: this is not just a treasure run. It is a rescue mission in a world already being lost.

---

## What Victory Means

Winning a run is more than clearing rooms and collecting loot.

It means pushing the invaders back, destroying one of their strongholds, and buying more time for the Kweebecs to survive.

The war for Orbis is still ongoing, but every rift you close matters.
